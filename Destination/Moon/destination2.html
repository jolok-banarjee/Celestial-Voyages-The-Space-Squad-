Overview

Moon tourism is now a reality, and people from all over the world are flocking to the lunar surface. The first tourist flights to the Moon were expensive, but the cost has come down significantly in recent years. Now, anyone with enough money can experience the thrill of visiting another world.

Things to Do

  • Take a walk on the lunar surface.
  • Explore the lunar craters and mountains.

Location

Pick-up: Wallops Flight Facility, Virginia, USA.

Description of Resort

The Lunar Grand Resort is located in the Mare Tranquillitatis, a vast plain that is considered to be one of the most hospitable places on the Moon. The resort is built underground, protected from the harsh lunar environment.

Resort Activities

  • Take a walk on the lunar surface and take in the stunning views of the moon.
  • Explore the lunar craters and mountains.
