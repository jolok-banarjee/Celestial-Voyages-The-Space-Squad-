Solar System

Our Solar System is a vast and complex celestial arrangement consisting of the Sun at its center and a multitude of celestial bodies orbiting around it. The primary constituents include eight major planets: Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune.
